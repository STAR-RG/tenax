We used the csindexbr score to agregate the data. The histograms below show the frequencies of csindexbr scores for each CNPq level (e.g., PQ-1A). The definition of score follows that of csindexbr (see their FAQ for details). To sum, papers in venues from tier 1 worth one full point, papers in tier 2 worth 0.4 point, and papers in tier 3 worth 0.33 point. The score of an individual consists of the sum of these points since 2013. It is worth noting that (1) only very few venues fall under tier 1, (2) venues in tier 3 are still pretty good, and (3) journals and conferences are covered (although tier 2 is exclusive for journals). Informally, the selection criteria of csindexbr is more inclusive compared to that of csrankings, but still very rigorous.
