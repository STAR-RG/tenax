Tenax

Tenax is a tool to visualize the production of CS Brazilian researchers at highly-selective venues (since 2013). It uses csindexbr data and CNPq data for that. Tenax does not mine data and does not define custom metrics. The primary goal of Tenax is to incentivize Brazilian researchers to publish at highly-selective venues. This is important, for example, to increase international visibility of BR-CS research, to reduce cost, and to raise credibility on our work. Interested users are advised to read the csindexbr FAQ to understand the venue selection criteria. For a summary of results (anonymized and grouped by CNPq levels), click here.
